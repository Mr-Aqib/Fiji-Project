Brief descriptions of each tea to appear next to the tea box picture

Turmeric and Cardamom Tea

This tea is very soothing and gently takes the stress out of any tiring long day. Steep for 3-5 minutes in boiling water. Enjoy it with a splash of milk and a spoon of honey or just plain. It is a very comforting tea for the evening after dinner.

Cocao Tea

This tea is made form the nibs and husks of the cocao beans we use in our chocolates. It has a light cocoa flavour and a bit of a kick in the morning instead of coffee. Best served plain or with the sweetener of your choice. A great way to kick start your day.

Lemon and Ginger Tea

This blend of ginger and lemon is a very vibrant combination that will tantalize your taste buds. It’s excellent as a hot beverage if your feeling a bit under the weather or a cold iced tea for a refreshing afternoon drink on a warm day. It’s quite invigorating and can be served with or without your favourite sweetener. We recommend honey as it marries and smooths the flavors of the ginger and lemon.

Mint and Moringa Tea

We’ve blended these two herbs to give you a delightful refreshing cup of tea. This tea is excellent as a hot beverage or try it as iced tea for a refreshing beverage on a hot day. Serve either plain or with your preferred sweetener.

Turmeric Tea

Our turmeric tea has a very strong earthy flavor. It’s quite suitable in the evening ater a long day sweetened with honey or your favourite sweetener. To get the maximum benefit of the curcumin found in turmeric add a small amount of black pepper and a drop or so of coconut or sunflower oil. The health benefits of the curcumin will be absorbed into your body more effectively by adding these two ingredients.

Ginger Tea

If you like snappy, exotic flavour of ginger you will love our ginger tea. Served hot with hiney it is excellent for a sore throat or upset tummy. As a cold drink it is spicy and deliciously refreshing with a hot spicy kick to awaken your taste buds instantly. Hot or cold our ginger tea is exceptionally flavourful.
